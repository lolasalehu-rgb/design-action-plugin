# Design Backlog

Living, scored backlog for design work across all streams.
Updated as tasks complete or new items are discovered via scans and briefings.

## Scoring

Scoring dimensions are configured in `~/.design-action/config.yaml` under `scoring.dimensions`.
Default: User Impact (40%) + Business Value (30%) + Effort (20%, inverted) + Strategic Alignment (10%).
